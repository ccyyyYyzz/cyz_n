> **Fundamental-gate underdetermination theorem.**
>
> Start from the type-II bulk, F-string, and democratic D-brane actions,
> optionally completed by covariant string-field interactions and the proposed

> absolute-rate CTMC (18), corrected full chemistry (24)--(27), and

> target-keyed controller, and it supplies a falsifiable full coupled spectral
> test.  Nevertheless:

> * if every adjacent finite-width rate is positive, no exact rank-three
>   stratum is forward invariant;

>   primitive;

> * one-time orientation and universal all-field cone alignment do not follow
>   from rank/chemistry dynamics.

> pole/residue constraints, while leaving species, exact rank phase, signature,
> ordered time, and universal carrier underdetermined.
