# Decision 0008 — adopt as a conditional underdetermination theorem

Response 0008 is **adopted with major narrowing**.  It is not a universal
no-go theorem for microscopic \(3+1\) selection.  Its durable result is a
conditional identifiability boundary for the audited source/action family:
the fixed-dimension GKM collision kernel plus unresolved local completion
factors does not determine a unique rank mode, an exact rank-three invariant
phase, a stringlike primitive orbit, an ordered time orientation, or the
project's strong all-field common cone.

## Adopt

1. For a finite birth--death chain, if every adjacent \(B_s,D_s>0\), the
   chain is irreducible, its stationary law has full support, and no proper
   rank subset is forward invariant.  Rank three can then be a concentration
   mode, but not an exact invariant phase.
2. The stationary ratio
   
   \[
   \frac{\pi_{s+1}}{\pi_s}=\frac{B_s}{D_{s+1}}
   \]
   
   and its strict crossing criterion are the correct finite-chain mode test;
   zero mean drift is insufficient.
3. A positive finite-width average of the GKM fixed-impact kernel cannot turn
   itself into the sharp clearance table used as a control in response 0007.
   The source studies wound-string annihilation at fixed \(d\), not a
   stochastic transition \(d\to d\pm1\).  See
   [GKM 2009](https://arxiv.org/abs/0908.0955) and the explicitly conditional
   fluctuation scenario in
   [GKM 2012](https://arxiv.org/abs/1212.2115).
4. A common bulk metric does not by itself imply the project's strong common
   operational cone.  Sector-dependent fluxes produce inequivalent
   open-string metrics, consistently with
   [Seiberg--Witten](https://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/9908142).
5. Continuous nondegenerate symmetric carrier forms preserve inertia.  An
   index-one attractor from arbitrary signature sectors therefore needs a
   degeneracy/singular transition or a restricted initial domain.
6. A Lyapunov function that factors through variables omitting signature,
   flux, orientation, or visibility cannot have the complete target stratum
   as its exact zero set.
7. The species question should be formulated using a duality-covariant
   primitive stringlike charge orbit and an orbit gap, not the frame-dependent
   literal label \(p=1\).

## Adopt only conditionally

1. The finite-width incomplete-gamma integral is exact for the declared
   normalized impact ensemble, but it integrates a controlled asymptotic GKM
   kernel; it is not an exact microscopic rate.
2. The core/outer error formula needs volume weights.  If
   \(f=(b_*/r)^m\) and \(\Gamma_{\rm ann}\) is the normalized annulus average,
   a consistent form is
   
   \[
   \left|\Gamma_{\rm full}^{\rm exact}
   -(1-f)\Gamma_{\rm ann}\right|
   \le (1-f)\epsilon_{\rm eik}\Gamma_{\rm ann}+f\nu_{\rm core}.
   \]
3. Product-Poisson stationarity is valid on each closed irreducible class
   under the complex-balance hypotheses of
   [Anderson--Craciun--Kurtz](https://arxiv.org/abs/0803.3042).  A conserved
   fiber \(Ln=Q\) need not itself be irreducible.
4. The arbitrary-mode theorem applies only to the broad completion class in
   which the unresolved rank-local factors can be varied independently.  It
   is not a theorem about a fixed completed microscopic action.
5. The two-mode pole/residue formulas are useful, but the bare location
   \(z=-\Omega\) is a transfer zero only for \(g\ne0\).  At \(g=0\) it is a
   removable point.  Moreover, the defect-like residue scales as
   \(g^2/(\Omega-a)^2\), so a bare-rate interpretation and observability must
   be balanced with an explicit detection threshold.
6. Pre-quotient observability remains the correct architecture, but the port
   matrix \(W\) and its zero-kernel property are supplied rather than derived.

## Reject in the present form

1. Equations (5)--(7) do not yet derive the CTMC.  They contain continuous
   degrees of freedom, while the displayed generator omits the corresponding
   drift.  No scaled Born--Markov/Kramers convergence theorem or error bound is
   supplied.
2. The action-to-rank interface is still manual.  Both of the following are
   compatible with the cited fixed-\(d\) source: annihilation changes only
   winding occupation, or annihilation also resets a release projector.  The
   source does not choose the latter.
3. Identical coordinate potentials give permutation symmetry, not general
   \(O(D)\) rotation equivariance; charge-dependent masses and vertices also
   obstruct unrestricted species relabeling.  Theorem 2 needs explicit
   equivariant parameter and mark-kernel hypotheses.
4. Theorem 8 is false as written.  A scalar retarded kernel
   \(\theta(t)e^{-\gamma t}\) has zero Onsager port-antisymmetric part yet
   carries a directed input--output record.  The valid narrower statement is
   that a time-reversal-symmetric action and equilibrium state do not by
   themselves select retarded rather than advanced boundary conditions.
5. The rank generator and response matrix are distinct operators.  Sharing a
   subset of parameters is a joint-consistency test, not a same-spectrum
   theorem.
6. The type-II/F1/D-brane starting point already assumes a Lorentzian bulk and
   worldvolume time.  It can study why three spatial directions become large
   inside a supplied \(9+1\) setting; it cannot by itself select one time
   direction.

## Consequence for the next executable

Do not append further direct jumps to the projector rank.  Build a joint
stochastic--continuous winding/geometry model in which:

1. GKM events change only source-authorized winding occupations;
2. anonymous scale factors and the shifted dilaton evolve under the same
   declared gravity equations;
3. the existing Green/anonymous/scale stack reads the visible rank from the
   response, rather than receiving rank as a microscopic jump variable;
4. the computation measures a three-dimensional visible plateau, leakage,
   attraction time, and non-three-dimensional predecessors;
5. every departure pole is computed from the resolvent of that same joint
   generator;
6. time orientation is treated as a retarded/advanced boundary or
   non-equilibrium entropy-current selection problem.

The fundamental gate is therefore not closed.  Response 0008 is retained as
a negative design specification that prevents a source collision rate from
being silently promoted into a dimension-changing law.
