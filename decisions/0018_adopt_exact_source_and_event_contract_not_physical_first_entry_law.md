## Adopted facts

1. The principal source is one ambient delta--Liouville measure. It is not an
   arbitrary mixture of normalized branches and not an unweighted surface
   measure on the constrained shell.
2. At zero world-sheet momentum and \(E_*>0\),
   \[
   \left(\frac{s_0}{E_*},\frac{s_1}{E_*},\frac{s_2}{E_*}\right)
   \sim\operatorname{Dirichlet}(4,16K-1,16K-1).
   \]
3. The canonical \(K=1\) audit cell has \(E_*=1\), \(d=16\), and radial
   shape \((4,15,15)\). It is a software-and-measure cell, not a unique
   cosmological preparation.
4. The direct Gamma-and-sphere sampler is an exact sampler of the registered
   normalized source law. The hierarchical-Beta construction is an
   independent radial implementation control.
5. Source validity is an outcome label. Every draw is retained, and changing
   the validity predicate cannot change coefficients or sample count.
6. Rank, normal geometry, response and reaction data are downstream of the
   source and event-selection map. They cannot condition either stage.
7. Every event record has exactly one primary outcome. Exceptional,
   censoring, cluster, geometry, rank and history properties remain typed
   orthogonal data.
8. A complete tie is a cluster-valued outcome. It has no physically
   authoritative lexicographic scalar representative.
9. Finite-window no-entry, global no-entry, ambiguous earliest ordering and
   unresolved root coverage are distinct outcomes.
10. The event proof-model registry binds source state, problem data, domain
    and image coverage, roots, geometry, clusters, rank marks and episode
    history into one replayable interface.
11. A Brief 0018 physical-looking solver record remains non-authoritative.
    Independent Brief 0019 replay is a mandatory part of the physical
    certificate.
