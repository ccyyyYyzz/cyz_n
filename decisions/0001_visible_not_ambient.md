# Decision 0001 — visible dimension is the estimand

Date: 2026-07-22

Unrestricted ambient dimension is not an identifiable property of registered
intervention-response data.  The program will target visible dimension and
signature relative to an intervention algebra, bandwidth/resolution and
declared model class.

This is not merely cautious language.  Blind-product models give an exact
non-identifiability theorem, and weakly coupled extensions give its finite
resolution counterpart.
