# Decision 0004 — adopt the regular-branch lift with layered completeness

Date: 2026-07-22

## Decision

The algebraic core of response 0003 is adopted. Let

\[
P=\mathbb R[z_1,\ldots,z_g],
\qquad
\eta_D:P\twoheadrightarrow\widehat{\mathcal A}_D,
\qquad
N_D=\ker\eta_D,
\]

and let a separately reconstructed event-branch presentation be
\(A=P/I\). Consistency of the event interpretation with every saturated
context gives \(I\subseteq N_D\). At a common physical character with
centered maximal ideal \(\mathfrak q_\lambda\), the canonical comparison is

\[
\Theta_{x,2}:
\frac{P}{I+\mathfrak q_\lambda^3}
\twoheadrightarrow
\frac{P}{N_D+\mathfrak q_\lambda^3}.
\]

Its kernel is exactly

\[
\ker\Theta_{x,2}
=
\frac{N_D+\mathfrak q_\lambda^3}
     {I+\mathfrak q_\lambda^3}.
\]

Therefore equality of the two local relation spaces, equality of their
Macaulay ranks and injectivity of \(\Theta_{x,2}\) are equivalent. If the
source local ring is regular of dimension \(d\), surjectivity makes

\[
\Theta_{x,2}\text{ an isomorphism}
\iff
\dim\widehat J_{\widehat x}^2
=\binom{d+2}{2}.
\]

This is the adopted Artin-to-event bridge.

## Three independent completeness certificates

The positive Paper-I theorem must keep the following logically separate.

1. **Context completeness.** A saturated substitution congruence and complete
   commuting gate action are supplied either directly or by an independent
   response-realization bound, reachability, observability, flat Hankel
   extension and gate-invariant nullspaces. A finite product cutoff does not
   prove all-depth closure.
2. **Relation and target-carrier completeness.** Registered generators exhaust
   the declared visible branch algebra, the relation ideal has a degree bound,
   and the physical panel is relation-unisolvent. For a branch-relative claim
   the target ideal is explicitly

   \[
   I=I(\overline{B_{\rm phys}}^{\,\rm Zar}).
   \]

   If the target includes several components, state and panel completeness
   must cover every declared component.
3. **Physical branch completeness.** The certified physical region \(B_0\) is
   a relatively open connected component of the selected regular real locus,
   is covered by finitely many refined response-open Nash charts, and is
   realized by the registered state/continuation class. Algebraic coverage and
   physical realizability are different claims.

None of these certificates implies either of the other two. In particular,
the response order \(n_J\) is not a bound on relation degree, Krull dimension
or physical coverage.

## Required corrections to response 0003

The following statements are not adopted without correction.

1. The executive and final theorem omitted target-carrier/Zariski alignment
   from their main hypotheses. Their unconditional output is branch-relative,
   not a complete global event algebra.
2. A nonzero Jacobian minor gives local Nash coordinates only after shrinking
   or refining the principal open into injective response-open cells; it need
   not make the whole principal open one global chart.
3. The strict local-jet notation is

   \[
   A_{\mathfrak m_x}/(\mathfrak m_xA_{\mathfrak m_x})^3
   \simeq A/\mathfrak m_x^3.
   \]

4. Fixed total-degree unisolvence is invariant under affine generator changes.
   A nonlinear polynomial automorphism must transport the filtration or enlarge
   the degree bound; arbitrary polynomial invariance at fixed degree is false.
5. The finite-noise theorem does not yet prove that all interval-consistent
   candidates share one presentation. A cross-candidate presentation-
   separation/equivalence certificate is additionally required.
6. A noisy relation subspace is estimated by the selected top-\(r\) right
   singular subspace with an explicit spectral gap. The row space of a
   generically full-rank noisy matrix is not that estimator.
7. The response's exact paraboloid toy validates the comparison after complete
   multiplication matrices have been supplied. It is not an end-to-end proof
   that finite context depth has already saturated.

The positive implementation must also run the non-dense \((xy)\) branch,
non-pure dimension-jump and delayed-context shift-chain controls.

## Consequence for Paper I

After these corrections, a reconstructed regular four-dimensional branch and
an isomorphic comparison map justify applying the adopted jet-null theorem on
the actual branch quadratic fiber. Rank nine with homogeneous centered sources
then identifies one conformal principal line. Multi-field factorization,
tangent-overlap gluing and directed time orientation remain separate downstream
gates.

The next bottleneck is no longer existence of a conditional event branch. It
is one cross-candidate, finite-noise, end-to-end certificate composing every
gate without latent coordinates in the inference interface.
