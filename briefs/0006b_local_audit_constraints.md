## 1. Selection certificate has been repaired

A positive Lyapunov derivative and the absence of a known hiding map are not
enough. Genuine selection now requires:

1. gap-free uniform \(3+1\) reconstruction on a nonzero scale interval;
2. a resolved non-\(3+1\) predecessor in the flow direction;
3. a positive-radius open basin;
4. a uniform decrease bound together with a worst-case arrival budget
   \[
   V_{\max}\le\gamma_{\min}L_{\min},

   and forward invariance of the target stratum;
5. a target non-encoding audit bound to the same dynamics family;
6. a positive injective/non-hiding visibility proof with zero kernel upper
   bound, also bound to that family.

Missing visibility evidence is unresolved, not non-hiding. A numerical
tolerance may not fill a scale interval for which no reconstruction
certificate exists. Compactification therefore remains stability/hiding even
if a nominal basin and decreasing quantity are supplied.

## 2. Local mechanism verdicts already established

### Brandenberger--Vafa

The source-faithful rate slice from arXiv:0908.0955 is

\[
\log\Gamma_W
=\varphi+2\log R-\log4
+(d-3)\log\frac{2\pi R}{\sqrt{\pi\Delta x^2}}
-\frac{|b|^2}{\Delta x^2},
\qquad
\Delta x^2=4\log R^2.
\]

For \(d=3\), the impact dimension vanishes and \(\Gamma_b=1\). For \(d>3\),
suppression is conditional on large impact; at \(b=0\) the prefactor can
enhance the rate. This is presently the strongest explicit
three-versus-more rate seam, but time, a type-IIA nine-torus, the
Hagedorn-to-radiation fluctuation and homogeneous minisuperspace are inputs.
It is a conditional spatial selector, not the full answer.

### Published graph models

Quantum Graphity examples choose target valence and loop spectra; the
Kelly--Trugenberger ensemble is already \(2d\)-regular and its action/scaling
use \(d\). Neither supplies a one-time multi-probe common cone or an open
dimension-blind basin. Treat the published forms as negative controls. Only a
new dimension-blind graph dynamics may re-enter.

### CDT, tensor/GFT and nearby random geometry

The completed primary-source audit found no published cross-dimensional
\(3+1\) selector. Four-dimensional CDT/EDT and restricted spin foams use
four-dimensional building blocks; fixed-rank tensor/GFT models use rank or
valence as a dimension label. Their spectral/Hausdorff flow is useful but
occurs inside the chosen dimension sector. The canonical tensor model is the
only retained test platform because one tensor framework can represent several
fuzzy \(S^n\) geometries, but the published calculations start near
dimension-specific \(SO(n+1)\)/harmonic templates and do not compare their
weights or produce a local common Lorentz cone. If you choose a random-
geometry route, it must be a new template-release mechanism, not a citation to
fixed-\(D\) dimensional flow.

### Lorentzian IKKT

The April 2026 result is a seed-accessible phase at
\[
N=128,\quad\gamma=4,\quad m_f=6,\quad\widetilde d=5,\quad\xi=12,\quad
\eta=0.005.
\]
It is initialized from a thermalized \(\widetilde d=3\) bosonic
configuration; a random start gives an unbroken result, and the authors say
complex Langevin cannot identify the dominant saddle. The action already
contains ten matrix labels and one Lorentzian sign, the production deformation
contains explicit anisotropy, and boost postprocessing is non-holomorphic.
There is no demonstrated large-\(N\), seed-free, deformation-removal path.
No paper-specific public code, raw data or full run card was found.

Therefore IKKT is the strongest pre-geometric reserve, but it does not
currently outrank BV on evidence for why three directions win.

## 3. Independent witness correction

Do not infer dimension from the first nonzero mixed response order. Three-wave
scattering relations exist in every fixed admitted dimension in the relevant
nonlinear inverse literature. The retained quantity is instead the minimum
number of independently certified codimension-one clean fronts whose direct
interaction locus is an independently separated point. Only then does
\[
\dim\bigcap_i\Sigma_i=d-k=0
\]
give \(d=k\). Carrier type, transversality, direct/cascade separation, subset
completeness and handle injectivity must be proved without using the
cotangent-rank result being checked.
