## Central object

On one common nine-direction microscopic state space, define

\[
 {\cal K}_x(d\tau,dm,c\mid{\cal H}_t)
 =H_x(d\tau,dm\mid{\cal H}_t)\,p_c(x,m),
\]

where \(H_x\) is the absolute first-encounter/recollision-time and mark law,
\(m\) contains every predeclared local encounter mark, and \(p_c\) is the
conditional probability of reaction channel \(c\).  Keep the absolute clock,
the normalized mark law and the channel probability distinct.

Use only pre-classifier quantities: charge/species, tension, the full compact
metric, warp/dilaton values, relative tangent and orientation, velocity,
impact parameter, compact position, local world-sheet state and declared
history variables.  The kernel may not read a visible count, `target_rank`, an
active mask, a later response threshold or any proxy fitted after ranks are

## Required construction

Choose one concrete string sector and controlled compactification regime.
Then supply all of the following.

1. **Microscopic state and initial law.**  Give one positive-support,
   rank-unconditioned \(S_9\)-exchangeable law, its measure and its physical
   preparation.  If it is proposed rather than source-derived, say so and
   list the independently measurable parameters.
2. **Absolute encounter process.**  Derive the encounter intensity and mark
   pushforward from the same microscopic flow/ensemble, or give a finite
   measurable closure with an exact source-limit theorem.  Do not choose a
   transverse dimension after seeing the output rank.
3. **Conditional channels.**  Insert the appropriate JJP/GKM conditional
   probabilities only on their valid source strata, including compact
   localization, velocity, angle, impact and species dependence.
4. **History and recollision.**  Retain a finite history state or prove a
   renewal/Markov reduction with a quantitative error.  State the
   recollision-time distribution and orientation correlations.
5. **Reverse reactions and energy.**  Derive the reverse marked channel from
   microscopic reversibility/detailed balance in a declared reservoir, or
   state an independently measurable reverse law.  Give every energy quantum,
   degeneracy factor and reservoir reset.  A free constant reverse edge is
   insufficient.
6. **Finite executable reduction.**  Give an exact finite state or validated
   interval model, a genuinely closed residual ledger, absolute rate bounds,
   cemetery rules and a finite-horizon approximation error.
7. **Anonymous response replay.**  Give complete finite \(A,R,B,C,M\) matrices
   and normalization rules, derive every response cell before cardinality is
   counted, and use the same operator for probability and Green/product
   responses.
8. **All-rank stability statistic.**  Compare all ten ranks under one law,
   band and error model using entrant strict residence, strict no-exit
   residence, conditional retention and worst leakage.  Average occupancy is
   diagnostic only.
9. **Controls.**  Replay the exact six-state cycle of Response 0011 as an
   abstract occupancy control, the current supplied-label CTMC gate, and the
   same physical law for candidate ranks two, three and four.
