# Brief 0000 — Initial architecture commission

Status: completed before repository initialization

Design the first rigorous bridge toward explaining why the visible world is
\(3+1\)-dimensional. Start from finite, noisy, restricted active responses and
do not preload coordinates, topology, adjacency, dimension, metric, signature
or a causal set.

The proposal must:

- prove unrestricted ambient dimension non-identifiable;
- construct an operational quotient and differential event germ;
- distinguish response realization rank, control dimension, event dimension,
  propagation rank and ambient dimension;
- recover or exclude a characteristic cone and Lorentz signature;
- support certified-within-class / class-excluded / inconclusive outputs;
- compare response spectra, characteristic reconstruction and nonlinear
  wavefront interaction order;
- provide formal theorem candidates, a non-circular toy and a two-week kill
  test.
