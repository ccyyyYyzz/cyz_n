# Brief 0022 — visible \(3+1\) synthesis, cone/signature closure and the inherited-time boundary

Baseline: the final verified Brief 0021 commit.

## Mission

Compose the complete microscopic chain with the non-circular response
reconstruction developed earlier in the project.  Decide what has actually
been established about observed \(3+1\):

1. whether restricted interventions reconstruct a four-dimensional visible
   quotient;
2. whether its spatial rank three is selected by the source-derived dynamics;
3. whether a Lorentzian cone and one time orientation are uniformly visible;
4. which parts are derived and which are inherited from the microscopic
   model.

The final statement must distinguish an operational theorem about the visible
quotient from an ontological theorem about the ambient world.

## 2. Operational quotient

Let \(\mathcal I\) be the preregistered class of interventions and
\(\mathcal O\) the registered response observables.  Two microscopic states
are operationally equivalent when

\[
x\sim_{\mathcal I,\mathcal O}y
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathbb P_x(O\mid I)=\mathbb P_y(O\mid I)
\]

for every \(I\in\mathcal I\) and \(O\in\mathcal O\), within the declared exact
or calibrated equality.

The visible object is the quotient

\[
\mathcal V=\mathcal X/{\sim_{\mathcal I,\mathcal O}},
\]

not the microscopic state space \(\mathcal X\).

The intervention and observation classes must be fixed before comparing
dimensions.  Enlarging the class may refine the quotient and expose compact
or internal structure; this is a prediction, not a contradiction.

its first/two-jet realization without supplying a coordinate count.  Let

\[

be the certified response-covector module after quotienting the
gate-invariant nullspace.

\[

all candidates \(m=0,\ldots,10\) or the full registered range.

## 4. Spatial/time split

The radius dynamics of Brief 0021 supplies a candidate three-dimensional
spatial residence state.  The response reconstruction must independently
identify a one-dimensional causal complement.

Let \(Q_x\) be the response-derived principal biform on
\(\mathsf T_x^*\mathcal V\).  Certify:

\[
\operatorname{rank}Q_x=4,
\qquad
\operatorname{inertia}(Q_x)=(1,3)
\]

up to the registered overall sign convention.

The spatial subspace is not defined as “the three radius directions.”  It is
the definite subspace determined by \(Q_x\), and its overlap with the
radius-visible response module must be proved.

The causal line is not chosen by a coordinate name.  It must be selected from
the retarded response support, semigroup order, energy-flow orientation or an
equivalent preregistered causal observable.

## 5. Cone and time orientation

For every regular cell, define the visible null cone

\[
\mathcal N_x=\{\xi\ne0:Q_x(\xi,\xi)=0\}.
\]

The cone package requires:

- nondegenerate Lorentzian inertia with a positive spectral margin;
- overlap covariance of \(Q_x\);
- preservation of the cone under chart changes;
- a consistent choice of one cone component;
- a cocycle test for time orientation;
- robustness under the full calibrated intervention/noise class.

An interval eigenvalue enclosure touching zero is unresolved.  Numerical
diagonalization with a tolerance is not an inertia certificate.

If a global time orientation fails but local Lorentzian cones pass, report a
locally Lorentzian, non-time-orientable visible quotient rather than
silently choosing signs.

## 7. The inherited-time audit

The finite-\(K\) F1 model used in Briefs 0018--0021 starts with a Lorentzian
evolution parameter and equations that distinguish it from the nine target
cycles.  Therefore it cannot, by itself, explain why the microscopic theory
has exactly one time direction.

The final response must label separately:

### Derived within the model

- the source-to-event probability law;
- the rank-blind encounter mixture;
- any certified selection of three response-visible spatial directions;
- the visible four-dimensional quotient;
- the operational Lorentzian cone/signature, if reconstructed without a
  coordinate-count input;
- the stability and plateau-loss boundary.

### Inherited assumptions

- the existence of a Lorentzian evolution parameter in the microscopic F1
  dynamics;
- the sign convention of the microscopic kinetic theory when it enters the
  response biform;
- the target-space and low-energy truncation used by the model.

Reconstructing one visible causal line is scientifically meaningful, but it
is not an origin theorem for time when one microscopic time was assumed.

## 8. Ambient non-identifiability

Even a complete visible certificate does not prove that the ambient ontology
is four-dimensional.

Run at least the established compact-dimension/internal-tower countermodel:

- below the registered threshold, compact modes are evanescent or absorbed
  into a finite effective response;
- the same visible data can be reproduced by a four-dimensional theory with
  internal fields;
- above threshold, a Kaluza--Klein ladder or profile harmonic may become
  visible.

The correct conclusion is:

> The declared interventions identify a \(3+1\)-dimensional Lorentzian
> visible quotient.

It is not:

> The ambient world has no additional dimensions or internal realization.
