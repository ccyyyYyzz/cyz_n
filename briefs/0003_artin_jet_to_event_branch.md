## Adopted input

Raw typed contexts reconstruct a finite contextual algebra
\(\widehat{\mathcal A}_D\), a physical character \(\widehat x\), and the
Loewy quotient

\[
\widehat J_{\widehat x}^2
=\widehat{\mathcal A}_D/
\widehat{\mathfrak m}_{\widehat x}^{\,3}.
\]

Every consistent event model has a filtered surjection

\[
J_{M,x}^2\longrightarrow\widehat J_{\widehat x}^2.
\]

A dimension squeeze or empty two-jet alias system certifies when it is an
isomorphism. Centered source-module gates give \(\beta_x=0\) for the conformal
branch. The jet-null theorem then identifies a four-dimensional projective
principal line when \(\operatorname{rank}H_x=9\).

## Exact bottleneck

The finite represented algebra is Artinian. It cannot itself have a regular
positive-dimensional character neighborhood. Its filtered layers may match a
regular germ through order two while a cubic or higher relation makes every
lift singular.

We need the strongest non-circular theorem that promotes the contextual Artin
object to the measured two-truncation of a genuine regular physical event
branch.
